About Tiệm Sách Nhỏ

"Tiệm Sách Nhỏ" was founded in 2024 by three passionate individuals in Ho Chi Minh City, with the vision of creating a cozy online bookstore that brings readers closer to their favorite books.

Our mission is to make books accessible to everyone, providing a variety of genres and delivering a unique experience for book lovers across the country. We believe that books can change lives, and we are here to make them available to you.
